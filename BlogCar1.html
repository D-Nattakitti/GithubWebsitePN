Sales Engineer - Job Description and Responsibilities

   • Responsible for strategizing and planning sales to government organizations and private companies within assigned territories.
   • Set objectives and sales plans, and participate in marketing planning to adapt to sales strategies.
   • Engage in government tenders and prepare bidding documents.
   • Develop plans and strategies to expand the customer base in the market area.
   • Contribute to the development of training and educational programs for the sales team.
   • Coordinate with the Implementation team to meet customer requirements.

QUALIFICATIONS

   • Male/Female, Age 25 – 35 Years
   • Bachelor’s Degree or higher in Engineering, IT, Computer Telecommunications, or a related field
   • Strong experience in the position and sales with government organizations
   • Excellent negotiation skills
   • Ability to work as part of a team
   • Strategic thinker, proactive in presentation skills, and effective problem solver
   • Able to work under pressure with responsibility and a service-oriented mindset
