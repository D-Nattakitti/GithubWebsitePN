WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2004 and have built ourselves into an established IT solutions provider in Thailand.

Over the years, we have primarily served Thai financial institutions, as well as government departments and agencies, predominantly in the administrative, telecommunication, and utilities sectors. Having established a strong relationship with BAAC since 2006, we have expanded our client base to include other government departments and agencies. We have successfully managed numerous projects across Thailand's utilities, military, administrative, and judiciary sectors.
